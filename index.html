Hi Everyone! My name is Christopher Valencia, but the casual “Chris” works for me. I am a graduate Engineering Alumni\Student from the University of Pennsylvania with a background in Mechanical Engineering, Surveying, and an onlooker regarding Web Design. Personally, I have always been fascinated by the way physical systems work in unity—whether it’s a car, industrial art, or a piece of program code that is a method for visualizing our everyday logic or simply displaying our skills on the "web." When I am not home, you can find me either at a local gym or at local design events wherever I can find one. I want to live my life as a constant learner in faith and with the support of others, and through these eyes, I am super excited to exchange experiences and working with you!
