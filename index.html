Hey Everyone! My name is Christopher Valencia, but the casual “Chris” works for me. I am a Senior at the University of Pennsylvania with a major in Mechanical Engineering, specifically concentrating on Energy, Fluids, and Thermal Systems and possible minor in Computer Science. Personally, I have always been fascinated by the way physical systems work—whether it’s a mechanical system like a car or a piece of code that allows me to get my home 3D Printer to make an idea I have into a material reality. When I am not home, you can find me either at a local gym or at local salsa dancing events wherever I can find one. I want to live my life as a constant learner, so I am super excited to exchange experiences and working with you!
